About the Show

"Unchartered Waters" takes you beyond the boardroom and into the mindsets of Africa's most successful leaders. Each episode features an intimate, unscripted conversation exploring the questions that matter most:

On Wealth & Alpha
"If you could give your younger self one piece of advice from the start of your career, what would it be?"
On Wellness & Fitness
"What's one non-negotiable part of your wellness routine that keeps you performing at your best?"
On Community & Culture
"Which community or relationship has had the greatest influence on your career trajectory?"
On Media & Influence
"Who has most shaped your mindset and approach to leadership?"

The show strips away the veneer of perfection to reveal the real challenges, decisions, and philosophies that drive success in emerging markets.
